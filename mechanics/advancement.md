# Advancement

Characters advance by spending XP on new perks or higher action ratings.

---

## Earning XP

XP comes from three sources.

### 1. Failure

When any roll results in **1–3**, mark 1 XP. This applies to all rolls: action rolls, resistance rolls, assist rolls, Devil's Bargains.

This is the primary driver of advancement. Characters with lower action ratings fail more often and advance faster in those areas — the rate naturally slows as ratings improve.

### 2. End of Session

At the end of every session, run through the following questions.

**Personal (each player answers for themselves):**
- Did you act on your Specialism in a way that created real risk for the crew? If yes, mark XP.
- Did your relationship with a crewmate, NPC, or faction meaningfully shift? If yes, mark XP.

**Group (everyone marks XP for each "yes"):**
- Did the crew complete or meaningfully advance an objective?
- Did the crew take on a threat bigger than themselves?
- Did the crew's reputation change — with anyone?

Maximum per session: 2 personal XP + up to 3 group XP.

### 3. Specialism Triggers

Each Specialism has a specific trigger that marks XP when you do your defining thing in a way that matters to the fiction. These are listed on each Specialism entry.

> Specialism XP triggers are not yet written. Each should follow the pattern: doing your Specialism's core action in a way that creates an opening or consequence for the crew.

---

## Spending XP

XP can be spent at any time — there is no downtime gate.

| Advancement | Cost |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 perk | 6 XP |
| Tier 2 perk | 12 XP |
| Tier 3 perk | 18 XP |
| Action rating +1 | 6 XP |

You must meet any prerequisites listed on a perk before purchasing it.

> Open question: Is there an XP discount for perks within your Specialism? See `mechanics/perks.md`.

## Notes

**Crew triggers (from `core-mechanics.md`) are not listed here.** It is not yet decided whether crew triggers give individual XP to each character or operate differently. Resolve this before finalising the full XP economy.
