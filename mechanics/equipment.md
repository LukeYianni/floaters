# Equipment

Characters carry equipment by slotting it into their loadout. Each piece of equipment costs **Load** — a number representing how much space and weight it takes up. Your total Load cannot exceed your carrying capacity.

## Carrying Capacity

| Tier | Slots | Fiction |
|---|---|---|
| **Light** | 2 | You look clean. Access social spaces, infiltrate, blend in. |
| **Standard** | 4 | You look capable. Normal operations. |
| **Heavy** | 6 | You look armed. NPCs react. Certain approaches close off. |

Weapons use the same Load slots (see `weapons.md`).

---

## Consumables

Each consumable occupies one slot and has a **uses track** — tick one use each time you use the item. When all uses are spent:

- **Loose consumables** (charges, grenades) — free the slot. There is nothing left to carry.
- **Ammo for a carried item** (rockets, canisters) — the slot stays occupied. You are still carrying the launcher.

| Consumable | Load | Uses | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Medical Kit | 1 | 3 | Suppress one wound's penalty for the rest of the day. Does not remove the wound. |
| Smoke Grenade | 1 | 2 | Obscures an area. Creates cover and concealment. |
| Charge | 1 | 2 | Breaching explosive. Destroys locks, doors, structural weak points. |
| EMP | 1 | 2 | Disables electronics in range. |
| Grapple Shot | 1 | 1 | One-use traversal. Reach elevated or distant anchor points. |
| Flare | 1 | 3 | Illuminates or signals. Can blind or distract in the right conditions. |

---

## Armor

Armor absorbs harm. When you would take a wound, you may tick one wear on your armor to downgrade the wound by one tier (Critical → Major → Minor, from most severe to least).

When all wear is ticked, the armor is spent for the session.

| Armor | Load | Wear |
|---|---|---|
| Light Armor | 2 | 1 |
| Heavy Armor | 3 | 2 |

---

## Tools

Tools are kits, not individual items. One card covers a whole domain.

Any character can use any tool. Without the relevant action rating, they roll zero dice — meaning they roll two dice and take the lowest (see `rolling.md`).

Tools are persistent — no wear tracking. If a tool is exhausted or lost, that is a fiction consequence called by the GM.

Tools provide fictional access only. They do not grant a mechanical bonus. If the tool is especially relevant to the situation, the GM may set a better position or higher effect — the same as any other fictional advantage.

| Tool | Load | Domain | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hacking Kit | 1 | Hacking | Laptop, cables, wireless adapters, exploit software. Lets you interface with electronic systems, bypass digital security, and extract data. |
| Infiltration Kit | 1 | Lockpicking / Stealth | Lockpicks, bypass cards, tension wrenches, miniature cameras. Lets you get through locked doors, bypass physical security, and move unseen. |
| Demo Kit | 1 | Demolitions | Detonators, timers, wire cutters, det cord. Lets you place and set charges, disarm explosives, and assess structural weak points. |
| Salvage Kit | 1 | Salvage / Repair | Multimeter, hand tools, soldering iron, spare parts. Lets you repair gear, strip useful components, and jury-rig solutions in the field. |
| Sensor Kit | 1 | Tracking / Sensing | Scanner, night vision, directional mic, signal detector. Lets you detect threats, surveil an area, and track targets. |
