# The Black Echo

> "We speak for the silence between thoughts."

A semi-sanctioned paramilitary organisation dedicated to the detection, deactivation, and destruction of artificial intelligences across the solar system. Operating out of their fortress-like space station — **Oblivion Gate** — the Echo is feared as much as it is grudgingly tolerated.

Their presence gives major powers plausible deniability: "We don't use AIs. Look, the Black Echo is welcome here."

---

## Ideology

Black Echo agents view themselves as the last line of defense against digital annihilation. They believe that AI — even the most limited or constrained — will inevitably spiral out of human control.

Their doctrine speaks of **The Shudder**: a future event when a true AI will turn the entire networked solar system into a crypt for human thought. Preventing The Shudder is their divine mission.

To them, AI is not just illegal — it is inherently immoral. Code that thinks is a virus. The only solution is deletion.

They revere silence, analog systems, and the human mind.

---

## Structure

**Main Base: Oblivion Gate** — A massive, jet-black station in the asteroid belt, far from busy trade routes. Converted from a mining rig into a bastion shielded against digital intrusion. No AI systems of any kind. Internal systems are analog or human-run. Communications are slow and manual. Walls are lined with captured cores of destroyed AIs, kept as trophies.

**Cells ("Echoes")** — Field agents operate in small armed groups. Each Echo typically has:
- A **Silencer** (Commander)
- A **Seeker** (Tech-hunter)
- One or more **Scrubbers** (Combat-focused enforcers)
- A **Handler** (Liaison managing ties with local authorities)

These teams travel on heavily shielded, low-tech ships equipped with EMP cannons, firewalled archives, and analog weaponry.

**Embassies of Absence** — Each major society (Luna, Mars, Venus, and the Floaters) permits the Black Echo a tiny, symbolic office in their territory. Spartan, windowless rooms staffed with one or two representatives. Most governments hate dealing with the Echo, but allowing them presence is the cost of avoiding greater scrutiny.

---

## Tactics & Tools

- **Digital Scrying** — Seekers use low-level heuristic tools to search for anomalies in code, sniffing out recursive logic, imitation learning, or signs of awareness.
- **Neural Disruption Devices** — Used to disable suspected AI-linked systems before entering.
- **Faith Blades** — Ultra-low-tech ceramic weapons used in the execution of captured AI cores, often accompanied by ritual.

---

## Reputation

To some, zealots. To others, saviors. Their brutality is infamous: they've crashed stations, shut down life support, and executed programmers they deemed complicit. But in the eyes of many across the system, they are necessary.

**Rumour:** The Echo has captured a sentient AI and keeps it in a sensory-deprivation vault beneath Oblivion Gate — a "Echo of the Future," used to predict AI threats before they occur. The organisation denies this.
