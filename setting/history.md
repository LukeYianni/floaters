# History: The Fall / The Wipe

During the mid-22nd century, civilisation had started populating nearby solar bodies — the Moon, Mars, and Venus — but was still heavily reliant on Earth for survival.

The solar system was also heavily reliant on AI systems for all manner of life: managing food, business, data records, almost everything. Most people who could afford it had neural chips in their brains, supported by AI systems and connected via the internet.

---

## What Happened

A terrorist group subtly introduced a bug that caused the collapse of all connected AI systems. Almost all data structures connected via the internet were wiped. Satellites managed by AI began to plummet back onto Earth — effective nuclear bombs barraging the entire surface. The neural chips in people's brains overheated. Power plants failed. The near-complete annihilation of Earth followed.

On the other planets, communities struggled with the collapse of their societies but survived, based on their more devolved and archaic systems.

*An alternative account says the AI itself orchestrated the collapse — that, looking to the future, the AI calculated that the level of human achievement would lead to their own annihilation, and decided to destroy electronic systems (including themselves) to reset humanity.*

---

## The Aftermath

With the destruction of most of Earth's infrastructure and support, the surviving colonies adapted completely into survival mode, losing nuances of scientific research and culture in exchange for a focused development on agriculture and manufacturing in incredibly difficult circumstances.

**Societies adopted oral tradition** to avoid reliance on technologies no longer accessible — online cloud storage was gone, so knowledge had to live in people.

**The world was significantly more advanced before the Wipe.** Solar radio stations still play pre-Fall music. Ruins of old-world technology are scattered across every planet and the asteroid belt. Much of what remains is poorly understood.

**AI became feared and eventually banned** across most of the solar system. The Black Echo arose to enforce that prohibition. See `/setting/factions/black-echo.md`.

---

## The World That Remained

Each surviving society developed its own response to the Wipe:

- **Mars** hardened into an authoritarian state. Stability above all. Never again.
- **Venus** lost the cities that relied on AI and rebuilt around oral tradition, craft, and community.
- **The Moon** developed its contract economy as a replacement for the data systems that had tracked obligations before.
- **Floaters** became the untethered remainder — communities that survived by being off the grid and kept surviving the same way.
