# Rejected Ideas

## Stress System
Replaced by Edge/Flow.

Reason:
- Encouraged caution and resource hoarding
- Required downtime to recover
- Did not support forward momentum

---

## Generic Shared Resource for +1d
(A shared pool used for assisting and bonuses)

Reason:
- Created player friction (“who spends it?”)
- Encouraged passive consumption
- Reduced importance of individual contribution

---

## Rewarding Failure Directly
(e.g. gaining resources when failing)

Reason:
- Created perverse incentives
- Encouraged intentional failure

Instead:
- Reward risk and action, not outcome
