# Glossary

Authoritative definitions for all Floaters system terms. When a term appears in any other file, its meaning is defined here.

---

## Edge

A personal, short-term resource representing immediate opportunity and individual momentum.

- Each character holds **0–2 Edge** at a time.
- Gained by taking risks, acting under uncertainty, or acting in ways consistent with your playstyle.
- Spent for personal benefit (+1d to your own roll, create a small advantage) or converted to Flow.
- Resets: no automatic recovery — must be actively earned.

---

## Flow

A shared crew resource representing team synergy and collective momentum.

- Tracked **0–6** for the whole crew.
- Gained by individual players converting their Edge into Flow.
- Spent to activate Flow abilities or trigger Flow State.
- Represents the crew functioning as a coordinated unit, not just individuals acting in parallel.

---

## Flow State

A special mode that activates when Flow reaches 6.

- Triggered by spending all 6 Flow (resetting to 0).
- Lasts for one scene or several actions (duration is an open question).
- Effects: Assist is free, +1d on team actions, Flow abilities unlocked.

---

## Crew

The player group as a collective unit. Each crew has a **type** (Rogues, Rebels, Scavengers, Hunters, Operators, Smugglers) that defines:
- A **crew Edge trigger** — a specific situation where the crew gains Edge (once per scene unless escalation).
- An **XP trigger** — the kind of accomplishment the crew is rewarded for.

---

## Playstyle

An individual character's role and approach. Defines:
- Personal **Edge triggers** — reliable, identity-specific ways to generate Edge.
- The character's narrative niche within the crew.

Playstyle triggers follow the formula: **Risk + Identity + Immediate Action**.

---

## Domain

A character's area of expertise or knowledge. Status: not yet fully integrated into mechanics. See `open-questions.md`.

---

## Assist

Helping another character on their roll. Mechanics are not yet fully defined. During Flow State, Assist is free.

---

## Desperation Rule

When a character acts with no advantage, no support, and faces high consequence, they gain **1 Edge before the roll**. Rewards committing to hard situations.

---

## Edge Trigger

A specific fictional condition that causes a character or crew to gain Edge. All triggers require a real fictional change — planning, waiting, and safe actions do not qualify.

---

## XP Trigger

A specific type of accomplishment that earns a crew XP. Requires real danger or consequence to activate.

---

## Core Loop

The cycle of play: take risks → earn Edge → spend Edge personally or convert to Flow → spend Flow for crew effects → earn XP through crew behavior.
